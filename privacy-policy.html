Privacy Policy for Lifestyle Utility Hub

Effective Date: February 1, 2025

1. Information Collection

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

1.1 Personal Information

  • Contact Information (name, email address)
  • Account credentials (if you create an account)
  • Communication preferences

1.2 Non-Personal Information

  • Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, click patterns)
  • Cookies and tracking technologies (see our Cookie Policy)

2. Use of Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To personalize content and advertising
  • To analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • To communicate with users about updates and offers
  • To prevent fraud and enhance security

3. Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display ads on our website. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website and other sites on the Internet.

3.1 Google Advertising Features

  • Remarketing with Google AdSense
  • Google Display Network Impression Reporting
  • Demographic and Interest Reporting

Users can opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers (analytics, hosting)
  • Advertising partners (in anonymized form)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

5. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • SSL encryption
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA regulations, you have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Opt-out of data processing
  • Lodge complaints with authorities

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@lifestylehub.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services do not target users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete it immediately.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via email or website notice. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
