Cookie Policy

Effective Date: February 1, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember information about your visit, enabling:

  • Proper website functionality
  • Personalized experiences
  • Traffic analysis and improvements

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

Necessary for basic functionality:

  • User authentication
  • Security features
  • Session maintenance

2.2 Performance Cookies

Collect anonymous data for:

  • Traffic analysis
  • Error monitoring
  • Performance optimization

2.3 Functional Cookies

Enable enhanced features:

  • Language preferences
  • Layout customization
  • Third-party integrations

2.4 Advertising Cookies

Used for:

  • Personalized ads (via Google AdSense)
  • Ad performance tracking
  • Frequency capping

3. Specific Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics tracking 2 years
_gid Session differentiation 24 hours
cookie_consent Stores user consent status 1 year

4. Third-Party Cookies

We partner with:

  • Google AdSense for advertising
  • Google Analytics for traffic analysis
  • Social media platforms for sharing features

These third parties may set cookies through our site. We don't control these cookies - review their policies:

  • Google Privacy & Terms

5. Managing Cookie Preferences

5.1 Browser Settings

You can:

  • Delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies through browser settings
  • Use private browsing modes

Guide: aboutcookies.org

5.2 Advertising Opt-Out

For personalized ads:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • Digital Advertising Alliance

6. Consent and Changes

We use a cookie consent banner that:

  • Appears on first visit
  • Allows granular preferences
  • Stores consent choices

7. Policy Updates

We may update this policy to reflect:

  • New services/features
  • Legal requirements
  • Industry standards
